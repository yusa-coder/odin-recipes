Chocolate Cake

Description

This chocolate cake recipe is rich, moist, and decadent. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or any time you crave chocolate!

Learn how to make a chocolate cake from scratch with a simple frosting and enjoy baking a classic dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat for 2 minutes on medium speed.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Allow cake to cool completely before frosting or serving.
